Simon Durbridge

creativity through engineering

I am a DSP engineer (in research) by trade, and I will soon to be an academic researcher by occupation.

In the new year, I will be starting a PhD in electronic engineering at the University of York; where I will be studying the definition of disturbing noise, and the impact of noise on sleep quality.

This work will include coupling biometric sensors with IoT elements, generating and deploying (neural network) machine listeners, and creating feedback devices that can help people get better sleep.

I aim to suppliment my research with consulting work, and selling synthesizer modules.

If you are interested in my research, modular synthesizers, or hiring me as a consultant, please get in touch!
