creativity through engineering

I am a DSP engineer (in research) by trade, and I am soon to be primarily an academic researcher by occupation.

I will be starting a PhD in electronic engineering in the new year; where I will be studying the definition of disturbing noise, and the impact of noise on sleep quality.

This work will undoubtedly include the creation of networks of IoT biometric sensors, (neural network) machine listeners, and feedback devices in the form of analytics and noise maskers.

I will suppliment my research with freelance programming (development), and making synthesizer modules to order.

If you are interested in my research, modular synthesizers, or hiring me on a freelance basis for a development task, please get in touch!
